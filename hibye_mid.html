PRIVACY POLICY FOR MOBILE APPLICATIONS

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application 'HiBye_Mid' for mobile devices created by HIBYE ENTERTAINMENTS. The Application is designed to help manage institutes.

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

User Provided Information

The Application collects information you provide when you download and register. Registration is optional, but some features may be unavailable without registration.

When you register and use the Application, you may provide:

  • Your name, email, age, username, password, and other registration details.
  • Transaction details, including purchases, responses to offers, and application downloads.
  • Information provided when contacting us for support.
  • Credit card details for purchases.
  • Details entered into our system, such as contact information and project management details.

We may also use this information to contact you with important updates and marketing promotions.

Automatically Collected Information

The Application may collect certain information automatically, such as your mobile device type, unique device ID, IP address, operating system, browser type, and usage data.

Does the Application collect precise real-time location information of the device?

No, the Application does not collect precise location information.

Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

Yes. We share your information with third parties only as described in this privacy statement.

We may disclose User Provided and Automatically Collected Information:

  • As required by law, such as to comply with legal processes.
  • When disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or others' safety, or to investigate fraud.
  • With trusted service providers who adhere to our privacy standards.
  • If HIBYE ENTERTAINMENTS undergoes a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, in which case you will be notified of any change in ownership.
  • To advertisers and third-party networks as outlined below.

Automatic Data Collection and Advertising

We work with analytics and advertising partners to understand usage patterns and provide targeted advertising. These partners may collect certain anonymous data such as unique device IDs, mobile numbers, and non-precise location information. We use encryption to protect user anonymity.

If you wish to opt-out of targeted advertising, please see the “Opt-out” section below.

Feed and User Reporting

The Application includes features to report content or other users. When a report is submitted, we review and take appropriate action, which may include content removal or user suspension if deemed necessary. These actions are in line with our commitment to ensuring a safe and respectful environment.

User Account Deactivation and Deletion

If a user deactivates their account, it will remain deactivated for a 90-day period. After this period, the account will be permanently deleted, and all associated user information will be removed from the Application.

Permanent Deletion of User Data

When a user account is permanently deleted, all user data, including personal details, activity records, and transaction history, will be completely removed from our systems and will no longer be accessible within the Application.

What are my opt-out rights?

Opt-out of all information collection by uninstalling the Application: You can stop all collection by uninstalling the Application using your device’s standard process. You can also email us at hibye.entertainments@gmail.com to request opt-out.

Opt-out of targeted advertising: Manage your location and privacy settings from your device settings or within the Application where applicable.

Data Retention Policy, Managing Your Information

We retain User Provided data as long as you use the Application. Automatically Collected Information is stored for up to 24 months, after which it may be stored in aggregate. To delete User Provided Data, please contact us at hibye.entertainments@gmail.com.

Security

We prioritize safeguarding your information through physical, electronic, and procedural measures. However, no security system is foolproof, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. We will notify you of changes by posting here and via email or text. Regularly consult this policy to stay informed.

Your Consent

By using the Application, you consent to our processing of your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy, including the use of cookies and data transfer to the United States under U.S. privacy standards.
